# Code of Conduct

This project adopts the [Contributor Covenant, v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/) as its code of conduct.

## Our pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behaviour may be reported to the maintainer at <simon.filler@tu-dortmund.de>. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

The full text of the Contributor Covenant v2.1 governs this project; please read it in full at the link above for the complete set of community standards and enforcement guidelines.
